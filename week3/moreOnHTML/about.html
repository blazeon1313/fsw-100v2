My About Page

I was a Firefighter for 16 years. Fighting fires was a passion of mine. There is nothing to compare to 102ft flames.

I fought fires all over the US from rolling hills to steep mountains.

Moving on from fire was a very hard transition. I hit a very low spot in life. I even tried to commit suicide 3 times during my low.

I have been diagnosed with chronic suicidal tendencies and have an excellent group that I do call on when I start the spiral effect. My support group is not small and they know if I am calling it is to check in or to get my mind going back straight.

Thank You for visiting my about page!
