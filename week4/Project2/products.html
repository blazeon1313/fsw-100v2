HERE'S THE BEEF

We pride ourselves on raising top quality beef.

Grazing our cattle on grasslands to feeding grass hay through the winter.

Calf to sale our cattle is raised on high quality feed.

Our calving starts in mid March and ends by mid April.

The yearly branding is held mid May so we can turn the pairs out to pasture by June.

The cowboys pride themselves and treat the cattle just as they are their own.

Sun up to sun down the work continues on.
