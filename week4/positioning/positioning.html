The Dream Job

FIghting Fire

Boots on the ground, March 15,2020

Boots in the dirt. We grab our tools and huddle up to make a plan. There is no rush as we have learned mistakes come from just rushing into the inferno. We have the plan and start the hike to the fire. First engine on scene we have the fire. Feeling the heat the closer we get. The smoke is thick all we hear is the fire crackling in the distance. There it is through the smoke we see the flames of the beast. We survey the fire briefly then get our tools ready. Make an anchor point and start the dig.

The Call Comes In

Responding to the call, June 28,2020

The tones on the radio ring out. Our ears perk up and smiles grow on our faces. We know the we are about to head out. We grab our gear and overnight bags. Load up in the Engine and head out. Driving down the road looking for a smoke column. Then we spot it, there in the distance. We stop and pull out the maps for the area. Finding the beast route to take in. Our route is planned as we proceed back down the road. The smell of smoke is in the air. we know the time to work is getting near.

Firefighter Life

Day to day, March 20,2020

Being a firefighter was one of the most rewarding experiences I have had. The traveling alone made the career rewarding. The years of camping all summer long sleeping on the ground with a roaring fire close by. The excitement starts with a call out on the radio that sends the crew out to a blaze. Big or small the fire had to be fought. spending from just a few hours to spending months on a fire location. From moutain tops to the grasslands fire had no boundries.
